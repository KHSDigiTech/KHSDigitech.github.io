üêõ Common Problems & Solutions

  • Car spins uncontrollably: Reduce angular_damp and linear_damp values
  • Car doesn't turn when stopped: Make steering depend on forward velocity
  • Car slides too much: Increase friction or reduce drift forces
  • Car bounces off walls: Adjust RigidBody2D's bounce property
  • Car feels too heavy/light: Modify mass and force values
  • Camera follows too closely: Adjust Camera2D offset and smoothing
