üí° Development Tips

UI/UX Design Tips

  • Keep It Simple: Clear, readable fonts and intuitive button placement
  • Consistent Style: Use the same colors, fonts, and button styles throughout
  • Visual Feedback: Buttons should respond to mouse hover/clicks
  • Essential Info Only: Don't clutter the HUD with unnecessary information

User Testing Best Practices

  • Silent Observation: Don't help or guide players during initial testing
  • Ask Open Questions: "What did you think?" not "Did you like the jumping?"
  • Note Confusion: Where do players get stuck or confused?
  • Watch Actions: What players do is more important than what they say
  • Test with Different People: Get varied perspectives and skill levels
