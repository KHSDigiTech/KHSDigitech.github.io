Week 3 - Sprint 1

‚úÖ Achievements & Progress

Successfully implemented smooth player movement with acceleration and deceleration. Created a simple enemy that follows the player when in range. ‚ö° Challenges & Problems

Fixed collision issue by adjusting collision layers and masks properly. Found a great tutorial on Godot's collision system. üß† Key Learning Moments

Understanding the difference between collision layers and masks was huge - it solved multiple problems at once. Also realized that playtesting immediately after implementing a feature helps catch issues before they become complicated.

üé≠ Mood & Motivation
